About

Who We Are

We are a graphic design and web development company with a prescence in Florida, North Carolina, and Puerto Rico. Our team is equipped with all of the tools and technical/practical knowledge needed to turn your dream website into a reality.

Why We Do It

The internet has grown exponentially in the last decade. With millions of new websites, applications, and technologies, the amounts of services and products that can be successfully marketed and sold on the internet have also grown exponentially. However, as businesses' online prescence grew, so did their demand for faster and more secure payment portals, more reliable methods of shipping and inventory handling, better authorization and authentication systems for their users, and other tailored-fit custom features for their businesses' online websites, applications, or platforms.

This, in turn, led to an unparalleled rise in the amount of and complexity of programming languages, libraries, frameworks, and technologies available for web-development. And thus, the world of web-development has turned into a daunting jungle full of pitholes that can leave businesses of all sizes lost and confused.

How We Do It

We propose different typographies, color palettes, badges, and markups for our clients to choose. Once all of these components are individually chosen, they are seamlessly integrated in order to bring our clients' websites to life. Clients may, if they so choose, use a CMS to set up their online shop or content. Customability is unaffected by this, since Gatsby allows us to design a custom markup/design while fetching content from the clients' CMSs of choice. This arrangement allows us to create and design with no restrictions while clients can control their content.

If clients wish to add a special functionality or application to their websites, then different architectures with detailed pros and cons will be proposed. Once the design/cost features are settled, the functionality or application will be implemented into the website and deployed.
